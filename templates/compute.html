Computing for a Cause

Our goal for Trident is to provide a comprehensive collection of results for analysis by scientists and non-scientists world-wide.
You can help us accomplish this goal by participating in our volunteer grid computing project.
Validated results will be made immediately availble here for viewing and analysis and will help fulfill Danny Thomas's vision that
"No child should die in the dawn of life".

To participate:

  1. Register with us
  2. Download the grid computing manager application
  3. Point your grid computing manager at: http://trident.stjude.org/
  4. Enter your registered credentials when prompted
