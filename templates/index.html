Welcome!

Trident is a genomics interaction web service designed to present results from genome wide associations studies with a variety of pharmacogenomic phenotypes incorporating network analysis between different genomic measurements. Our currently featured tool is psi, the microRNA:dsDNA binding site algorithm. psi is a novel method for prediction of Hoogsteen type base pairings between microRNA and double-stranded DNA with intergration with in vitro data from primary patient samples. You can browse the results, make a prediction using your own input, or volunteer your computers idle time to compute for a cause. {% endblock %}
